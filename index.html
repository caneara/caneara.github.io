Developer, entrepreneur and content creator

Hey there… I'm Matt Kingshott, a full-stack developer, open-source enthusiast and content creator. In 2022, I formed Caneara to serve as an umbrella for my closed and open-source projects, as well as my client and consulting work.

I started my journey back in 1999 with Visual Basic, played around with C# for a few years, before finally settling on Laravel and PHP as my preferred platform. Since then, I've also come to know and love VueJS, Tailwind CSS, and most recently InertiaJS.

I've built, deployed and maintained client and SaaS projects on servers, and have recently dabbled with serverless via Amazon Web Services and Laravel Vapor. While still a little rough around the edges, it is fast becoming my preferred option.

While my workload is pretty full these days, I'm always open to the right challenge when it comes to consulting or client work, so please feel free to reach out to me if you're interested in the two of us working together.
